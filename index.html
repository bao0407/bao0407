Simulation of Starlink LEO Satellites with ns-3 and Optional DDoS Vulnerability Assessment

Abstract

This project proposal concentrates on simulating Starlink's Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO) satellite network using the ns-3 network simulator, focusing on evaluating performance across various scenarios. This research tackles significant challenges in optimizing satellite communication networks. Through simulation-based methodologies, the study seeks to offer important insights for improving the reliability and resilience of Starlink's satellite internet constellation before real-world implementation.
